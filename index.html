Every flower is a feeling

Across centuries, people have placed emotions into petals - love, sorrow, hope, forgiveness. Flowers can speak without words - you just need to understand their language.

This website is a small journey into the world of flowers and emotions. Browse the gallery, find out which flower reflects you, or simply get a wish for today.
